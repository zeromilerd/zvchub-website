NatiVault

NV is a brand-new, innovative, and unique entry into the hardware crypto wallet industry. It is built with the cutting-edge patent-pending cybersecurity solution, Zero Vulnerability Computing (ZVC). The technology has already gained support from 10 top-tier cybersecurity experts (of which 3 are EU's Cybersecurity Centres of excellence) and is currently being authenticated at Europe's premier research facility, IMEC, which is funded by the European Commission under the EU's Horizon 2020 programme.

NV: A Paradigm Shift in the History of Cybersecurity and Computers

NV represents a new paradigm in cybersecurity, convenience, and value by bringing and keeping the hardware wallet secure where it belongs: NATIVE- on the computer that processes your cryptocurrency.
"It is supported by a unique cyber-secure technology, ZVC, which makes it completely secure and efficient."
NV is more than simply a hardware wallet; it is a confirmation of a new computing paradigm that has the ability to usher in a new epoch in the history of computers.
